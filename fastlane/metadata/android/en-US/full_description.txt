NekoVideo is a modern, open-source video player for Android focused on simplicity and privacy.

Browse your video library organized by folders and subfolders, with full support for nested directories.

Features:

* Browse videos organized by folders and subfolders
* Supported formats: MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, WMV, M4V, 3GP, FLV
* Password-protected private folders (local AES encryption, no cloud)
* Background playback with media session and notification controls
* Gesture controls for volume, brightness and seek
* Video cutting and trimming (lossless, no re-encoding)
* Shuffle playlists from any folder
* Picture-in-picture (PiP) support
* Open With support for external video files
* Cast to DLNA/UPnP devices on the local network
* No ads, no tracking, no internet permission required
